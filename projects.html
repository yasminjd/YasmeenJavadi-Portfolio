About the App

STYA is an AI-powered fashion styling app that allows users to upload their photo and virtually try on new clothing, accessories, hairstyles, and hair colors. Users can style complete outfits by adding items from a personal library, save their looks, and move seamlessly from inspiration to purchase. The app combines creative exploration with practical shopping, offering free starter tokens and flexible subscription plans.
